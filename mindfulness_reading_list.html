I recently Tweeted, half tongue-in-cheek:

This has resonated with people. Many of you have suggested books that cover some of these topics well. Here is the list, in case you find it helpful. I suspect that none of these have The Answers, but that they all have Really Useful Tidbits.

Reading List for Focus/Flow/Mindfulness/Balance/Awareness

  • Peace is Every Step: The Path of Mindfulness in Everyday Life, Thich Nhat Hanh This was my first exposure to this concept. I've adopted a handful of everyday mindfulness practices from it that have changed me in ways too profound to plumb.
  • Flow: The Psychology of Optimal Experience , Mihaly Csikszentmihalyi
  • It Doesn't Have to Be Crazy at Work , Jason Fried, David Heinemeier Hansson
  • Rapt: Attention and the Focused Life , Winifred Gallagher
  • Make Time: How to Focus on What Matters Every Day , Jake Knapp, John Zeratsky
  • Essentialism: The Disciplined Pursuit of Less , Greg McKeown
  • Deep Work: Rules for Focused Success in a Distracted World , Cal Newport
